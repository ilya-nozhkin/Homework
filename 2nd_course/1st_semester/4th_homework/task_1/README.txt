How to start server:
1. Install python 3
2. $python3 server.py

How to connect:
1. Find text field on the bottom of page
2. Type <ip_address>:<port> of the server (server port is hardcoded to 25535)
3. Wait until second player connects too

How to play:
Left and right arrows - move your tank
Up and down arrows - change the gun angle
Shift - change type of ammo
Enter - fire

Move your tank to take a good position. Then select ammo, take aim at your enemy and fire.
Remember! You can't move into your opponent's side.
